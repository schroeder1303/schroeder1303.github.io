U.S. Hydro and Wind Power Plants

Patterns of energy production

5 facts about Hydropower:

  • 4 main types: run-of-river, storage, pumped storage and offshore
  • Approximately 60% of all renewable electricity is generated by hydropower
  • China, Brazil, USA and Canada are the largest hydropower producers
  • One of the cleanest sources of electricity
  • Although it has high upfront construction costs, hydropower provides low cost electricity over its lifetime
(International Hydropower Association)

5 facts about Wind power:

  • One of the largest source of renewable energy generation in the US
  • Helps avoid 340 million metric tons of CO2 emissions annually
  • Turbines will start to generate electricity with wind speeds at 6-9 mph
  • Typical wind turbine will repay its carbon footprint in less than 6 months
  • There are nearly 70,000 wind turbines across the country
(American Clean Power Association)
