Great Barrier Reef

This project shows the widespread reach of coral bleaching on the Great Barrier Reef (GBR). A recent study by scientists with James Cook University estimates that only 2% of coral from the GBR has avoided a bleaching event since 1998 and up to 80% has been severely bleached at least once since 2016.
