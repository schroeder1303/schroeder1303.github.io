Walking in Valladolid, Spain

About these spots

The stops made on this map were frequently visited by myself when I was a student at the Universidad de Valladolid in Spain from 2011-2012. Unlike Madrid, Valladolid does not have a metro system and instead relies on buses and cabs. Unfortunately for me, my apartment building was not on a bus line and so I spent most of my time walking. The spots on this map are some of my more frequently visited: my apartment, gym, class building, cafe, and grocery.
